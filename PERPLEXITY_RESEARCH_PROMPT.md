## Research Prompt

I need comprehensive research for a business presentation about **CallSense**, an AI-powered real-time call monitoring and emotion intelligence platform. Please provide detailed analysis on the following:

### 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
- What we built: Real-time emotion detection system for customer support calls using AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash for emotion analysis, Groq Whisper for transcription)
- Core features: Live emotion tracking (anger, frustration, satisfaction), instant transcription with speaker identification, AI-powered response suggestions
- Technology stack: Next.js, Supabase, Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, Groq Whisper, real-time audio processing
- Unique value: 2-second response time for transcription and emotion analysis during live calls

### 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
Research and quantify:
- Current challenges in customer support call handling
- Cost of customer escalations and poor satisfaction scores
- Impact of agent stress and burnout in call centers
- Statistics on customer churn due to poor support experiences
- Time wasted on post-call analysis and quality assurance
- Lack of real-time coaching for support agents

### 3. MARKET SIZE & OPPORTUNITY
Provide data on:
- Global customer support software market size (current and projected)
- Call center AI market growth rates
- Emotion AI / affective computing market size
- Number of customer support agents globally
- Average cost per support call
- ROI potential for AI-powered call monitoring solutions

### 4. TARGET INDUSTRIES & USE CASES
Deep dive into these verticals:

**A. Customer Support / Call Centers**
- Market size and pain points
- Average handle time (AHT) reduction potential
- CSAT score improvement statistics
- Agent turnover reduction opportunities

**B. Sales Teams**
- Inside sales call volume statistics
- Impact of emotion intelligence on close rates
- Sales coaching and training costs
- Revenue impact of better call handling

**C. Healthcare / Telemedicine**
- Patient communication challenges
- Regulatory compliance requirements (HIPAA)
- Patient satisfaction correlation with outcomes
- Telehealth market growth

**D. Financial Services**
- Call center volumes in banking/insurance
- Compliance and quality assurance costs
- Customer retention value
- Fraud detection opportunities

**E. Education / Student Support**
- Student support call volumes
- Mental health crisis detection
- Retention impact of better support

### 5. IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)
Define characteristics:
- Company size (employees, revenue)
- Call volume thresholds
- Industries with highest need
- Budget ranges for call center software
- Decision makers and buying process
- Current pain points and triggers

### 6. VALUE PROPOSITION
Quantify the benefits:

**Time Savings:**
- Reduction in average handle time
- Elimination of post-call analysis time
- Faster agent onboarding and training
- Automated quality assurance

**Cost Savings:**
- Reduced escalations (% and $ impact)
- Lower agent turnover costs
- Decreased supervisor intervention needs
- Reduced training costs

**Revenue Generation:**
- Improved customer retention rates
- Higher CSAT/NPS scores impact on revenue
- Increased sales conversion rates
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities

### 7. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
Research competitors:
- Existing call monitoring solutions (Gong, Chorus.ai, etc.)
- Emotion AI companies (Affectiva, Cogito, etc.)
- Traditional call center QA tools
- Our differentiation: Real-time (2s latency) vs post-call analysis
- Pricing comparison and positioning

### 8. BUSINESS MODEL
Provide benchmarks for:
- SaaS pricing models for call center software
- Per-seat vs per-minute pricing
- Typical contract values and lengths
- Customer acquisition costs in B2B SaaS
- Churn rates for call center software

### 9. GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY
Research best practices:
- Distribution channels for call center software
- Partnership opportunities (CRM, helpdesk platforms)
- Marketing channels with highest ROI
- Sales cycle length for enterprise software
- Pilot program structures

### 10. METRICS & KPIs
Industry benchmarks for:
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT) scores
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- First Call Resolution (FCR) rates
- Average Handle Time (AHT)
- Agent utilization rates
- Customer effort score (CES)
- Escalation rates

### 11. IMPLEMENTATION & ROI
Provide data on:
- Typical implementation timeline for call center software
- Change management best practices
- Time to value expectations
- ROI calculation frameworks
- Payback period benchmarks

### 12. FUTURE TRENDS
Research emerging trends:
- AI adoption in customer support (growth projections)
- Emotion AI regulatory landscape
- Privacy concerns and solutions
- Integration with other AI tools (chatbots, knowledge bases)
- Predictive analytics in customer support

Please structure your research with:
1. Executive summary with key statistics
